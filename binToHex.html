Hex to Binary Page

Why do we need to know how to convert Binary to Hexadecimal, and vice versa?

It is important to know how to convert between binary and hexadecimal, to better understand how the two are connected. This also helps us to understand how computers process colours, and all the little pieces inside those colours. Having a deeper understanding of this relationship makes us better computer programmers!

How do you convert Hexadecimal to Binary?

Converting hex to binary is simple! First, pull the charcters apart. Then, change any letters or other characters into their decimal counterparts. After that, convert your numbers into binary. You can do this by using the divide by 2 method. Be sure that you have 4 digits, so if your number less than that, add 0s to the front until you have 4. All thats left to do is squish all your numbers back together. I knew you could do it!

How do you convert Binary to Hexadecimal?

Wow, I love your dedication! First of all, pull the numbers apart in groups of 4, beginning with the furhtest right number, and working your way to the left. If the last group is less than 4, add 0s until it becomes 4 digits. Convert the groups into decimal numbers by adding the 1's matching base values together (for more information, see Binary page). If a number is greater than 9, then change it to the appropriate letter. (Just a reminder: A = 10, B = 11, C = 12) Now just squish the numbers back together! Wow, you're a natural!
